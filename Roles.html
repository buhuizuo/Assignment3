Roles

    our team has 2 members only so far, we basically both need to be project manager, product designer and developer ect. if in the future we are able to hire more people or recruit more members, we’ll separate roles, so we can all focus on our own task, here is a table of specific roles with different members and what exactly the role need to do.

Project manager

    Project manager is a person responsible for managing and leading the whole team. Their role is to efficiently optimise the work of the team, ensure the product is meeting the requirements and identify the goals for the team. 


skills, qualifications and experience

● Extensive experience in managing small to large complex technical projects in a large and complex service industry organisation, including relevant project management qualifications or equivalent.
● This includes extensive knowledge and demonstrated experience ensuring projects are completed on time, on budget, to quality standards and within agreed scope.
● Project Management Qualifications (PRINCE2, PMBOK, Agile) with extensive experience in project managing large/complex projects.
● Ability to adopt and adhere to Project Management Governance Intermediate proficiency in Microsoft Project and Visio or equivalent.


Software architect 

    Software architect is a highly-skilled software developer that has to think through all the aspects of the project and is responsible for making high level design choices, as well as select technical standards (for instance, determines the technology stack to use).

skills, qualifications and experience

● Automation of current workloads and monitoring services for advertising and operational team
● Develop orchestrated workflows for publishing content using AWS Elemental Media Services and popular AWS products Lambda, Step Functions, S3, CloudFront etc
● Experience in AWS development using cloud services and monitoring
● Experience in templating, step functions and CloudFront
● Experience in peer reviewed development with version control and CI/CD workflows
● Experience integrating third party API services with self-managed architecture
● Preference to experience in diagnosing issues on platforms using network tools like Charles Proxy, WireShark, browser developer consoles


Frontend Developer

     Frontend developer creates the part of your application that the users see and directly interact with. They make sure the experience is equally smooth and user-friendly to every person who uses the app.   The quality of literally everything your user interacts with depends on the skills of your frontend developers. The hardest part of their job is to make sure every single user gets the same experience – whether they use Edge or Chrome, computer or phone, Android or iOS. Frontend developers are trained to be browser charmers and masters of resolutions backend developer, Chief Technological Problem Solver. It’s the person who writes complex and sophisticated algorithms and application logic that makes all the magic happen under the hood. 

skills, qualifications and experience

● 4 years industry experience 
● Computer science degree or similar  
● Experience in developing applications for marketers 
● HTML / CSS / SASS 
● Web accessibility 
● Mobile-first development methodology 
● Progressive enhancement methodology 
● Web components (nice to have)
  ● Javascript / Typescript 
● React 
● NodeJS 
● NextJS (or other static site generators eg Gatsby) 
● GraphQL (CRUD) 
● REST API exposure 
● 3rd party API integrations (social, maps...) 
● Unit testing frameworks 
● Headless CMS exposure (Storyblok/GraphCMS) 
● Database design knowledge (writing intelligent schemas) 
● Ability to technically critique UX/UI design and provide quality feedback 
● AWS serverless infrastructure (s3 / Cloud Formation scripting / Lambdas) 
● Github/bitbucket pipelines 
● Scrum rituals 
● StorybookJS / StencilJS (nice to have) 


Backend developers

    Backend developer is responsible for everything that is under the hood of the application, and they are your chief problem-solvers. make sure product has an efficient, yet powerful engine. They plan and develop the whole application architecture, decide which services and databases should communicate together, how API and external integrations should work and how to make sure the product is secure and stable. 
They are consultants, not just coders.  A good backend developer not only implements what they are told to but also looks for the most efficient ways to solve a given problem. For example, if you ask your BE developer to implement a particular database, and the developer knows a different solution will work better for your app, they will surely convince you to consider another option. Sometimes the idea you have might be time-consuming to implement but the same effect can be delivered faster using a different approach – backend devs will know it. 

skills, qualifications and experience

● Java/ J2EE Full Stack Developer with Spring Boot, AWS, Git, Jenkins, Kubernetes
● Minimum 3-5 Years of Implementation experience in developing restful web services using spring Boot and Java 11.
● Strong experience in developing micro service using Spring Boot.
● Expertise in Java 8 and 11
● Strong experience in Spring Framework such as Spring MVC, IOC, AOP and Spring JDBC.
● Strong experience in ORM (e.g. JPA, Hibernate), SQL and developing REST APIs using Jersey framework.
● Experience in core Java-Multithreading, collections, Servlet and JDBC.
● Unit and integration testing using Junit 5 and Mockito.
● Knowledge of Apache Kafka and other massaging services.
● Working experience in Industry Standard protocols related API Security including OAuth is a must.
● Experience in AWS, Container Services (Docker, Kubernetes)
● Hands on experience with build and deployment tools such as GitHub, Jenkin Maven.


Quality Assurance Engineer

    QA Engineers make sure that your application is bug-free, has adequate support across all devices/browsers, and meets all the requirements.they spot the bugs and problems before your users do. While testing your application, QA specialist pays attention to performance, security, usability, portability, and looks of the application from the end-user perspective. They are not just testers, they make sure implemented features meet the requirements (which they help to describe beforehand anyway), write documentation, create test cases, and proactively contribute to establishing the best development practices in the project. Their role is critical in delivering the high-quality product, seamless end-user experience and fulfilment of project requirements. In the long run, not only are they time-savers, but also irreplaceable headache-savers.

skills, qualifications and experience

Over 5 years exposure to cloud infrastructure as code concept with expertise with cross device testing strategies and automation via devices
History of developing Test Strategies and Plans in conjunction with App Engineering, Architecture and Prod Arch. This includes Data creation and management, as well as Environment stewardship and management.
Excellent coding and analytical skills
Experience working in a TDD/BDD environment and can utilize technologies such as JUnit, Rest Assured, Karate, Jbehave/Cucumber frameworks, APIs (REST/SOAP)
Understand Continuous Delivery concepts and can use tools including Jenkins/Bamboo and vulnerability tools such as Sonar, Checkmarx/Fortify, etc


Product Designer

    Product Designers help you to transform your product vision into user-friendly designs and create user journeys optimised for the best user experience and highest conversion rates. Application development team roles require Product Designers. They are key players in making sure the product you build succeeds. People tend to think of them as graphic artists that create nice looking designs. While it’s true, Product Designers make sure the feel and look of your application and the overall user interface is as sharp and eye-pleasing as possible, it’s not their only responsibility. They will be your team members through the whole development process, from translating your business goals into functional user experiences, to analysing, evaluating and enhancing those experiences over time. 

skills, qualifications and experience

● A minimum of 3 years building and delivering digital products in product led B2B or B2C companies.
● The ability to balance business goals and user needs in the production of design solutions.
● A strong sense of typography, layout and space.
● Able to distil complex problems into simple and understandable solutions and then communicate those solutions to both leaders and peers.
● An online portfolio so you can show off to us your methodical thinking and beautiful design work.
● Knowledge of code
● Flexible work approach to support teams in different time zones.
● You are a great team player who is able to work collaboratively and find ways to “Be Better”.


Business Analyst(user requirement)

    they make sure you are building the right product at right time to solve the right problem, and that it’s in line with your overall business model and client’s expectations. Research shows that if 8 to 15% of the time and funds are spent on business analysis, then chances for project success grow significantly. A Business Analyst helps you to craft a product vision and create requirements based on your customers and stakeholders needs and later help you in validating if you are on the right track.

skills, qualifications and experience

● Strong communication skills – both written and verbal;
● Knowledge of best practice process mapping and analysis methods and standards (e.g. BPMN, Lean, Six Sigma, CMM);
● Competent in the use of process mapping tools i.e. Visio;
● Strong understanding of all aspects of the Project Management and system / software development lifecycles;
● The highest levels of personal integrity and ethical behaviour;
● Solution and customer orientation;
● Passion and drive continuous improvement;
● Knowledge in a Service Provider environment, ideally including Facilities Management experience;
